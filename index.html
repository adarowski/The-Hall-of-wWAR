About the Hall of wWAR

Weighted Wins Above Replacement (wWAR) aims to identify the best candidates for the Hall of Fame not just by total value, but also by peak value, postseason value, and other adjustments. While compiling great overall value is a great way to get into the Hall of Fame, producing magnificent single-season or postseason performances are other good ways. wWAR combines these into one score. Wins Above Replacement (WAR) data comes from Baseball-Reference.com.
