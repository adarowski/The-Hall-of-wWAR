wWAR Compared to Graham Womack’s Hall of Fame Project

Graham Womack has posted the results of his second annual Hall of Fame project. He collected 86 ballots where Hall of Fame fanatics voted for their 50 best players not in the Hall of Fame. Graham tallied the votes and compiled the consensus Top 50.

I thought it would be fun to see how the Top 50 looked by wWAR. So, below you will see the Top 50 as they appeared in Graham’s list (along with their number of votes out of 86 ballots). The width of the bar is their wWAR. The yellow background shows where the Hall of wWAR induction line lies.
